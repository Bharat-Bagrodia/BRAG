About Us 🔍

At Bagrodia Research and Analytics Group (BRAG), we believe in the power of research-driven insights to transform businesses. Our mission is to provide innovative solutions that help our clients make informed decisions and achieve sustainable growth.

With a team of experts specializing in product consulting, UI/UX design, user research, and brand strategy, we bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to every project. We are committed to excellence, integrity, and delivering results that exceed expectations.

Our vision is to be the leading research and analytics partner for businesses seeking to innovate, adapt, and thrive in an ever-changing market landscape.

Our Services ⚙️

Product Consulting 💡

We help you develop and refine your product strategy through market analysis, competitive benchmarking, and user feedback, ensuring your products meet market needs and business goals.

UI/UX Design 🎨

Our design experts create intuitive, engaging, and accessible user interfaces that enhance user experience, drive engagement, and build brand loyalty.

User Research 👥

Through user interviews, surveys, and usability testing, we gather valuable insights about your target audience, helping you create products and services that truly resonate with users.

Complex Problem Solving 🧩

We tackle intricate business challenges with a structured approach, combining data analysis, creative thinking, and industry expertise to develop effective solutions.

Focus Group Discussions 💬

Our moderated focus groups provide deep qualitative insights into consumer opinions, preferences, and behaviors, informing better business decisions.

Brand Innovation 📈

We help you build and evolve your brand through market research, competitor analysis, positioning strategy, and creative concept development.
